Frequently Asked Questions

To activate your game key:

  1. Log into your Steam, Epic Games, or other platform client
  2. Look for "Activate a Product" or "Redeem Code" option
  3. Enter your game key exactly as provided
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete activation

If you need help finding your keys, check your account dashboard under "Game Keys" section.

Our refund policy allows returns under the following conditions:

  • Request made within 14 days of purchase
  • Game key has not been activated/redeemed
  • For bundles, no individual keys have been redeemed

To request a refund, contact our support team with your order number.

If you haven't received your keys:

  1. Check your account dashboard - keys appear there instantly
  2. Check your email spam/junk folder
  3. Verify your email address in account settings
  4. Contact support if keys are still missing after 1 hour

Payment issues can occur due to:

  • Insufficient funds or credit limit reached

  • Incorrect billing information
  • Expired payment method

Try using a different payment method or contact your bank to authorize the transaction.
